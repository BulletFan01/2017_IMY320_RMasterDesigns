AXEL! is a third person adventure game that follows Axel through his adventures! Jump on top of houses, over platforms and face the adventure that awaits.

Info

Axel has his beloved guitar stolen and will do anything to get it back. Play around on various missions and tasks to help the locals and to get Axel his guitar back!
*AXEL! is a game designed by RMasterDesigns as a year project for the BIS(Multimedia) degree.
